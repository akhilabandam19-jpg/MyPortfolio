### Approach A: "Technical Brutalism" — Raw Engineering Aesthetic
- **Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism meets Technical Documentation
- **Core Principles**: Raw honesty, structured information hierarchy, code-inspired aesthetics, monochrome with sharp accent
- **Color Philosophy**: Off-white parchment (#F5F0E8) base, deep charcoal (#1A1A1A) text, electric amber (#F59E0B) accent — signals precision and craft without corporate polish
- **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric two-column grid with a fixed left sidebar for navigation, content flows right. Section dividers use thick horizontal rules. No rounded corners anywhere.
- **Signature Elements**: Monospace code blocks as decorative elements, numbered section headers (01, 02, 03), terminal-style command-line snippets in hero
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Hover states reveal underlining and color shifts. No animations — interactions are immediate and direct.
- **Animation**: Minimal — only fade-in on scroll for content sections
- **Typography System**: JetBrains Mono for headings + Source Serif 4 for body text
</text>

### Approach B: "Structured Elegance" — Editorial Engineering Portfolio
- **Design Movement**: Swiss International Typographic Style meets modern SaaS
- **Core Principles**: Grid discipline, typographic hierarchy, restrained color, information density with breathing room
- **Color Philosophy**: Deep navy (#0F172A) as primary surface, slate-white (#F8FAFC) for content areas, electric blue (#3B82F6) as the single accent — professional authority with technical credibility
- **Layout Paradigm**: Full-width sections with alternating dark/light backgrounds. Left-aligned text blocks with right-side diagrams/visuals. Timeline for experience. Cards for case studies.
- **Signature Elements**: Thin horizontal rule dividers, numbered case study cards, subtle grid overlay on hero section
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth scroll-triggered reveals, tab-based case study navigation, sticky nav with progress indicator
- **Animation**: Framer Motion slide-up on scroll, staggered card entrance, smooth tab transitions
- **Typography System**: Space Grotesk (bold, geometric) for headings + Inter for body text — clear hierarchy

- **Design Movement**: Dark Luxury meets Developer Aesthetic

## Selected Approach: B — "Structured Elegance"

Chosen for its professional credibility appropriate for client-facing work. The Swiss grid discipline and editorial layout signal engineering rigor. The navy/blue palette reads as trustworthy and technical without being flashy. Ideal for a senior engineer sending this to enterprise clients.
